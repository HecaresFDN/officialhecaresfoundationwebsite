Who We Are

HECARES FOUNDATION is a faith-based youth initiative focused on children, teenagers, and young people—helping them grow with moral clarity, spiritual depth, and responsible digital awareness.

What We’re Building

We are building a global youth education movement that blends faith, technology, and life skills—preparing young people for the future responsibly.

What We Do

Christian Education

Building faith, identity, and moral strength.

Digital Literacy

Understanding technology with clarity and responsibility.

Future Readiness

Preparing youths for ethical global participation.

About the Founder

David King Hecares is a young, passionate African visionary committed to building, strategizing, and supporting young people across the globe. His mission is to help youths grow in faith, develop moral clarity, and gain early awareness of technology so they can face the future with confidence, purpose, and readiness.
